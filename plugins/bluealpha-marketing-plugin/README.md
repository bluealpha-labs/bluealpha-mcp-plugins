# BlueAlpha Marketing Plugin

A Claude-powered toolkit for performance marketers running Google Ads. Talk to Claude in plain English and get back account audits, keyword strategies, geo expansion plans, audience reviews, creative refresh ideas, and full incrementality test designs — all backed by your live Google Ads data and BlueAlpha's marketing mix model.

No spreadsheets. No SQL. No agency lag.

## Who this is for

Performance marketers, growth leads, and in-house Google Ads owners who want a senior media strategist in their corner without paying senior-strategist hourly rates.

## What you can ask it to do

Once installed, just talk to Claude. Some prompts to try:

- *"Audit my Google Ads account and tell me what to fix first."*
- *"Find new geographic markets I should be testing."*
- *"My CTR is dropping — is it creative fatigue?"*
- *"Help me build a conquest campaign against Competitor X."*
- *"What audiences are wasting my budget?"*
- *"Design a geo holdout test to prove my brand campaign is incremental."*
- *"Run a full account analysis from top to bottom."*
- *"Turn this blog post into a paid campaign brief."*
- *"Where am I covered organically but missing on paid?"*
- *"Plan a counter-move — competitors just took 30% of my impression share."*

The plugin walks you through the answer, asks follow-up questions if it needs context, and produces strategy documents and campaign specs you can take straight to your team.
